Privacy Policy - Gold Signal Pro V12.20

Last updated: July 24, 2026

Summary: This App displays public financial data (prices, volumes, moving averages). We do NOT collect personal data, we do NOT provide financial advice. No Buy/Sell signals.

1. Information Collection

We do NOT collect personally identifiable info except name in Profile. Uses Firebase Cloud Messaging for informational push. Token stored locally.

2. Financial Disclaimer

For informational purposes ONLY. Public market data without advisory analysis. NOT financial advice. Trade at your own risk.

3. Data Sources

Price data from public APIs (OANDA, TradingView). MA50 and RSI are mathematical calculations, NOT recommendations.

4. Push Notifications

Informational only, e.g. "Price closed above MA50 at $XXXX - S/R Level". NOT Buy/Sell. Disable anytime.

5. Third-Party

Firebase (Google) and TradingView.
